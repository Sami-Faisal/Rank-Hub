Frequently Asked Questions

How do we start the project?
We begin with a discovery call to map requirements and priorities. I deliver a scoped proposal with milestones, timelines, and costs before any development starts.
What tech stack do you use?
I pick the right stack for the job — usually modern, battle-tested options (Node/Express, Python/Django, or .NET for backends; React/Vue for frontends; PostgreSQL/MySQL for databases). I explain choices in the proposal.
Will my data be secure?
Yes. I implement secure authentication, encrypted storage/transport, role-based access, and follow best practices for deployments. For payment processing or sensitive data, I design systems to minimize exposure and meet required standards.
Do you provide maintenance after launch?
Yes. I offer support and maintenance plans: bug fixes, updates, monitoring, and feature iterations. You can pick a monthly retainer or pay-as-you-go support depending on your needs.
